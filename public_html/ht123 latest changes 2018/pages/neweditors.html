New Editors & Submission Deadlines for the Haibun Section

Haibun Today is pleased and honored to announce the additions of Ruth Holzer and Melissa Allen to its haibun editorial staff. They will join the existing staff of Glenn G. Coats and Ray Rasmussen.

Ruth Holzer's haiku, haibun and tanka have been published in many journals and anthologies over the past decade. She has served as Assistant Editor for tinywords and written reviews for Haibun Today. Her longer poems have been gathered into three chapbooks including A Woman Passing (Green Fuse Poetic Arts). Retired from a career as a linguist, she lives in Virginia.

Melissa Allen's haiku, haibun, and other short poetry have been published in numerous journals and anthologies, including Haiku in English: The First Hundred Years (Norton, 2013). She writes a poetry blog, Red Dragonfly, has contributed reviews to many publications, and was haibun editor of the short-lived journal Multiverses. She lives in Madison, Wisconsin and, after many years spent working in bookstores, libraries, and archives, home educating her now-grown son, earning a graduate degree in library science, and writing freelance journalism, currently earns her living as a technical writer for a software company.

Submissions of no more than three unpublished haibun that are not currently under consideration elsewhere should be forwarded to our editors at our new common email address: hthaibuneditor@gmail.com, based on the following schedule:

March Issue: submissions accepted from Dec 01 to Jan 30 -> Primary Editor Melissa Allen
June Issue: submissions accepted from March 01 to April 30 -> Primary Editor Glenn G. Coats
September Issue: submissions accepted from June 01 to July 30 -> Primary Editor Ruth Holzer
December Issue: submissions accepted from Sept 01 to Oct 30 -> Primary Editor Ray Rasmussen
