Ship of Fools

Plunging backward off black rock into a lukewarm turquoise blue, at once we’re surrounded by hundreds of yellow tang, a school of fish so expansive that it brings to mind a field of swaying sunflowers. Black durgons, hawksbill turtles, and red pencil urchins fill in this undersea bouquet, but together they still can’t hide patches of coral bleached white as rice.

sushi boat
running aground on a strip
of plastic grass
