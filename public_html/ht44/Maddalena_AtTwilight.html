Victor Maddalena
St. John's, Newfoundland, Canada

 

At Twilight

On this sultry evening I sit on the veranda in the wicker chair and listen to the wind in the trees and the solitary cricket hidden in the long grass near the crab apple.  The air is heavy with the scent of lilac and wild rose.

In the distance, thunder . . .

bats
from the eaves
take flight
