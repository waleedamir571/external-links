Watermark

Rhododendron, kalmia, and hydrangea in bloom, and other herbaceous and woody plants, each with its spicy fragrance, unseen—the stone mansion overlooks the broad beach.

the first scion
within the gardener’s reach
a shard of sea glass

lake freighter
in the offing
a deeper blue

scrub cliff—
on weathered stairs
wellingtons
