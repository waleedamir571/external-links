Jeffrey Harpeng
Brisbane, Queensland, Australia

 

Disinter

how many white spots
on the butterfly’s wing
but one sun in the blue

a live cane toad
under a white bucket
but not a sound

talk of the afterlife
we cannot sing
a bird from the tree

in his painting
the scenery bleached
he talks of suicide

he slits his wrist
with a credit card
there is no blood

car crash scene
a butterfly mask’s
empty stare
