In Memoriam

Ruth Franke
4 May 1932 – 14 April 2011

Ruth Franke, born in Braunschweig, lived in Emmendingen where she practised haiku, haibun and ikebana. Mafora Verlag, in 2002, published her book of haiku, Lapis Lazuli, while 2010 saw the release, via Wiesenburg Verlag, of a bilingual German-English edition of her haibun,Schwerelos gleiten – Slipping Through Water.

Erste Narzissen
der Briefträger bringt
eine Todesanzeige

first daffodils
the postman brings
an obituary
