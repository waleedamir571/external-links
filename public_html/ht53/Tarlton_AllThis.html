all this shows an example

The stamp that is not only torn but also fitting is not any symbol. It suggests nothing. A sack that has no opening suggests more and the loss is not commensurate. The season gliding and the torn hangings receiving mending all this shows an example, it shows the force of sacrifice and likeness and disaster and a reason.

                                                                                    Gertrude Stein,Tender Buttons

 

just this rose
the one that's missing
several petals
making it stand out
irregularly red

Somewhere there's aRoblee's shoebox with the lid on it, wrapped and tied with string. There's an old wallet inside, with a coin flap that could snap shut, a black rubber comb, a half empty bottle ofFitch's rose hair oil, a pocket knife with a broken horn handle, two baseballs signed by Ted Kazanski and Del Ennis from the '55 Phillies, and a silver shoe horn. I don't know where the box is anymore; I assume it's lost. But, it's somewhere, and with all that stuff.
