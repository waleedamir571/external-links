Addis Ababa, Ethiopia

 

Far from Shore

Coming into shore, I see a hawksbill turtle and follow it for a while. Mirroring the turtle's movements as it glides beneath the surface, trying to match the movement of my arms with its forelegs, I lose track of time. A snorkel full of water brings me up, and I can see that I'm far from shore. Even through my prescription mask lens, the mountain ridges running down to the beach are no more than smudges.

cloudless sky one swell after another
