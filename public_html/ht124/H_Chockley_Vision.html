Vision Quest

Sitting in the deep cold of the cave beyond the reach of day or night, the only sound is the beating of her heart, resonant and powerful. Presently, she senses her russet hide, the powerful muscles of her shoulders and neck, and the long curving horns of her bison-spirit. Days of hunger and fervor follow. Using the shape of the cave wall, she blows ochre and soot black to form the charging bull. Her final sign, the soot halo of her shaman hand on the wall.
