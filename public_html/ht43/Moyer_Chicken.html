Francis Masat
Key West, Florida, USA

 

Chicken Dinner

The weathered door swings in the wind, squeaking back and forth, back and forth.  In a way, it invites me in, into the smells of cooking fat, wood smoke, and biscuits with black pepper gravy—though the scene has been gone for years now.

Mom’s cook-shack—
dark stains
on the butcher block
