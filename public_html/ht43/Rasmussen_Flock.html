Flock of Grimaces

Our instructor constantly encourages, and if that fails, nags us to smile while doing tai chi.

Balanced on one leg, arms raised gracefully, she looks enraptured while moving through ‘white crane spreads wings.’

A glance around the room reveals a huddle of penguins wobbling toward a distant ocean.

fledgling
a worm too big
to swallow
