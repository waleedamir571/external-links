Landscape

The sable brush smooths a line and a new road tears itself through the hills.

willy willy
across the playground
dusty children

The sky, was it ever still, coalesces into a line of clouds flowing over Constable pastures.

wisp of cirrus
the paintbrush smooths
a shoulder into sky

An old windmill fogs into a Turner sunset, transforms into the David Hockney blue of a California swimming pool.

diving board
a line of bubbles
floats to the surface

sparkling dew
through the gum trees
the horizon brightens
