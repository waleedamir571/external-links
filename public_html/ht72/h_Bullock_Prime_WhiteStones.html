White stones

white stones in the sun
after all

Thisness: the knowledge that we are each the matrix of human experience so far.

In my parent’s bedroom there is a dressing table with mirrors on three sides. Peering into one of the side mirrors, I see reflected over my shoulder an infinity of me, receding back in ever smaller rooms. Perfect recreations of reality, but as each grows smaller, bits of reality are lost and the image becomes a picture rather than a reflection.

insomnia—
louder than the clock
