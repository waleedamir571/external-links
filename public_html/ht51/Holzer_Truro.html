Truro

An easy drive from Halifax up to Truro, over the gentle hills. From the last rise, I can see the Minas Basin and the town along the river where I had hoped to watch the tidal bore. Too late—on the grassy banks, the tourists were already dispersing; I had missed the moment of high tide roaring in from the Bay of Fundy in a great foaming wall. It's all right, I'll be coming back again.
