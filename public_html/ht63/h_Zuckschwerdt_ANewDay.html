A New Day Awakens

Balmy air after last night’s storm. Early morning’s velvet robe. Birds are about to wake up. Whispers. The grass is wet with early morning dew. Sunflowers wait to turn their heads towards the sun. The lake is partly shrouded in soft white mist and the sky is deep blue with pink and red stripes.

After sunrise, birds start chirping and everything comes to life. Rush takes over.

stillness
birds sing softly
before sunrise
