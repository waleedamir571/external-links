Another Sunday Morning

“Do you think we should get more?” he asks no one in particular, eyeing the bagel selection behind the deli counter. One always liked egg bagels; the other favored plain. “Maybe they will stop by.” And I say sure, get two more. But I know they aren’t coming. They never visit on Sundays anymore. But he buys the bagels every week, just in case.
