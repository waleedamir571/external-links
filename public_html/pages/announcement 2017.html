Journeys 2017 Released

Journeys 2017 has just been released by Editor Angelee Deodhar and can be purchases through Amazon.

This is the third in the Journeys series devoted to printing the best of contemporary poets' published works. Each of the three volumes presents the work of a different set of poets.

In addition, the anthologies feature essays, excerpts from the work of Japanese masters and selections of works by early adapters of haiku and haibun.

Journeys 2017 has a total of 133 haibun, the work of 29 poets.
